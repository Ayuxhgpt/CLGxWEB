Unit I: Limit Tests

Principle of Limit Test for Chloride

Based on the reaction between chloride ions and silver nitrate in the presence of dilute nitric acid to form silver chloride precipitate.

Cl- + AgNO3 --(HNO3)--> AgCl (ppt) + NO3-
Key Reagent for Arsenic Limit Test: Gutzeit Apparatus using Mercuric Chloride paper.
