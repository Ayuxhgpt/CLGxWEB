For Students

How do I download notes?

Go to the Resources page, select your subject, and click the download button on the desired PDF.

Can I upload my own projects?

Yes, you can submit your project proposals via your Dashboard. A faculty member will review it within 48 hours.

Technical Support

The app isn't working offline.

Ensure you have installed the PWA. Try refreshing the page once when online to update the service worker cache.

How to contact developers?
