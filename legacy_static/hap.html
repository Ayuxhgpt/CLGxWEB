Unit I: The Cell

Structure of Cell

The cell is the basic structural and functional unit of life. Major organelles include:

  • Mitochondria: Powerhouse of the cell (ATP production).
  • Ribosomes: Protein synthesis.
  • Nucleus: Contains genetic material (DNA).
  • Golgi Apparatus: Packaging and secretion.
Mnemonic: "My Really Nice Green Car"
M = Mitochondria, R = Ribosomes, N = Nucleus, G = Golgi, C = Cytoplasm
